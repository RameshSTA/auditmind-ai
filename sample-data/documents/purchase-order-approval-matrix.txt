PURCHASE ORDER APPROVAL MATRIX

Applies to all purchase orders and vendor invoices processed through Accounts Payable, effective
Q1 2026.

TIER 1 — Under $10,000
Approver: Procurement Manager
Requirements: Purchase order on file, three-way match (PO, receipt, invoice) for goods; two-way
match (PO, invoice) acceptable for recurring services under an active master agreement.

TIER 2 — $10,000 to $50,000
Approver: Department Director
Requirements: Same as Tier 1, plus a documented business justification attached to the purchase
order.

TIER 3 — $50,000 and above
Approver: Controller, with CFO notification
Requirements: Same as Tier 2, plus competitive bid documentation (minimum two quotes) unless the
vendor holds an exclusive/sole-source designation on file with Procurement.

RECURRING VENDOR PAYMENTS
Vendors under an active master agreement (e.g., Meridian Logistics, Sterling Facilities Management,
Acme Supplies) may be paid on a recurring schedule without a new purchase order per invoice,
provided the invoice amount is within 10% of the amount specified in the master agreement's rate
schedule. Amounts exceeding that variance are routed for Tier-appropriate approval as if they were
a new purchase.

DUPLICATE PAYMENT CONTROLS
The accounts payable system performs an automated check for invoices with matching vendor, amount,
and a transaction date within 5 business days of another paid invoice from the same vendor. Any
match is held for manual review before payment release. This control does not catch cases where a
vendor re-issues an invoice under a new invoice number for the same underlying shipment or service
period — reviewers should independently verify the underlying work or shipment reference, not rely
on invoice number alone.

Exceptions to this matrix require written sign-off from the Controller and are logged in the
exception register maintained by Accounts Payable.
