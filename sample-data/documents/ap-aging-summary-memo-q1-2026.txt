MEMO — ACCOUNTS PAYABLE AGING SUMMARY, Q1 2026

To: Corporate Controller
From: Accounts Payable Manager
Date: March 15, 2026

This memo summarizes the accounts payable aging position as of March 14, 2026, for the FY2026 Q1
Accounts Payable Audit engagement scope.

AGING SUMMARY
- Current (0-30 days): 78% of open payables by dollar value
- 31-60 days: 15% of open payables by dollar value
- 61-90 days: 5% of open payables by dollar value
- Over 90 days: 2% of open payables by dollar value, concentrated in two disputed invoices under
  review with Vendor Legal

VENDOR CONCENTRATION
Six vendors account for approximately 70% of Q1 payment volume: Meridian Logistics, Sterling
Facilities Management, Blue Ridge Consulting Group, Northwind Office Solutions, Acme Supplies, and
Vantage IT Services. This concentration is consistent with prior quarters and reflects the
Company's core operating vendor relationships rather than a new risk.

OBSERVATIONS
Aging remains within target ranges. The Over-90-days bucket, while small, includes both disputed
invoices flagged above; Accounts Payable recommends Internal Audit review the underlying dispute
documentation as part of the current engagement scope, since a disputed invoice held open for this
long increases the risk that a duplicate or replacement invoice gets paid without the original
being properly closed out.

Prepared by Accounts Payable, reviewed by the Controller prior to distribution.
