VENDOR DUE DILIGENCE CHECKLIST

Used by Procurement for every new vendor onboarding, prior to activating a vendor master file
record.

1. Legal entity verification (business registration, tax ID / W-9 or W-8 on file)
2. Bank account ownership verification (bank letter or voided check matching the legal entity name)
3. Sanctions and watchlist screening (OFAC and equivalent)
4. Related-party disclosure question answered by the vendor's Company sponsor
5. Certificate of insurance on file, where the vendor will be on Company premises or handling
   Company data
6. Data security questionnaire, for any vendor with access to Company systems or data
7. Two-person sign-off on the vendor master file record (Procurement Analyst + Procurement Manager)
8. Initial purchase order capped at $10,000 for the first 90 days of the relationship, regardless
   of the vendor's proposed contract value, to establish a payment history before higher-value
   purchase orders are approved

Any step skipped requires a documented exception approved by the Controller, logged in the
Procurement exception register.
