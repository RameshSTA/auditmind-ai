VENDOR MASTER AGREEMENT

Vendor: Acme Supplies LLC
Vendor ID: VEN-1042
Effective Date: March 3, 2025
Contract Term: 24 months, auto-renewing unless terminated with 60 days' written notice
Category: Office and warehouse consumables

1. SCOPE OF SUPPLY
Acme Supplies LLC ("Vendor") shall supply office consumables, packaging materials, and warehouse
supplies to the Company on an as-ordered basis under individual purchase orders issued against
this master agreement. No minimum purchase commitment applies.

2. PRICING AND PAYMENT TERMS
Pricing is fixed per the attached rate card (Exhibit A) for the first 12 months, subject to a
single CPI-indexed adjustment thereafter. Payment terms are net 30 from invoice receipt. Invoices
over $5,000 require a matching purchase order and receiving confirmation before payment release.
Invoices are to be submitted to accountspayable@company.example with the purchase order number
in the subject line.

3. APPROVAL AUTHORITY
Purchase orders up to $10,000 may be approved by a Procurement Manager. Purchase orders between
$10,000 and $50,000 require Director-level approval. Any single transaction at or above $50,000
requires Controller sign-off regardless of budget availability.

4. VENDOR CONTACT
Primary contact: Denise Coulter, Account Manager, Acme Supplies LLC
Billing contact: ap@acmesupplies.example

5. TERMINATION
Either party may terminate for convenience with 60 days' written notice. The Company may terminate
immediately for material breach, including repeated invoicing discrepancies exceeding 2% of total
annual spend.

Signed on behalf of the Company by the VP of Procurement.
Signed on behalf of Acme Supplies LLC by the Director of Sales.
