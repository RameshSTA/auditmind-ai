VENDOR MASTER AGREEMENT

Vendor: Meridian Logistics Inc.
Vendor ID: VEN-1017
Effective Date: January 15, 2024
Contract Term: 36 months
Category: Freight, courier, and last-mile delivery services

1. SCOPE OF SUPPLY
Meridian Logistics Inc. ("Vendor") provides scheduled freight pickup, courier delivery, and
warehouse-to-warehouse transfer services across the Company's three regional distribution centers.
Services are billed per shipment against the rate schedule in Exhibit B, with fuel surcharges
adjusted monthly.

2. INVOICING
Vendor invoices weekly, batching all shipments completed in the prior seven days into a single
invoice. Each invoice must reference the shipment manifest numbers it covers. The Company's
accounts payable team has flagged, in the past, that Vendor occasionally re-submits a batch invoice
under a new invoice number when the original is not paid within 10 business days — this practice is
a known source of duplicate-payment risk and is called out here so that any invoice review should
cross-check manifest numbers, not invoice numbers alone, before releasing payment.

3. PAYMENT TERMS
Net 15 from invoice date. Late payment does not accrue interest under this agreement, which is why
Vendor's collections team follows up quickly and, per section 2, sometimes re-invoices.

4. APPROVAL AUTHORITY
Standard weekly invoices under $2,000 are approved by the Logistics Coordinator. Invoices at or
above $2,000 require Logistics Manager approval and a matched shipment manifest.
