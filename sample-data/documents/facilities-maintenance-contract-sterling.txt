FACILITIES MAINTENANCE CONTRACT

Vendor: Sterling Facilities Management Inc.
Vendor ID: VEN-1029
Effective Date: June 1, 2025
Contract Term: 12 months, renewable annually

1. SCOPE OF SERVICES
Sterling Facilities Management Inc. provides janitorial services, HVAC preventive maintenance, and
minor facilities repairs across the Company's headquarters and two regional offices. Services are
billed monthly at a fixed retainer of $4,800, plus time-and-materials for repairs outside the
scope of preventive maintenance.

2. INVOICING
A single consolidated invoice is issued on the first business day of each month, covering the
prior month's retainer plus any time-and-materials charges, itemized by work order number.

3. APPROVAL
Monthly retainer invoices within 5% of the fixed retainer amount are approved by the Facilities
Coordinator without further review. Time-and-materials charges above $1,000 per work order require
Facilities Manager sign-off referencing the specific work order.

4. VENDOR CONTACT
Primary contact: Renata Ibarra, Regional Operations Manager, Sterling Facilities Management Inc.

Signed on behalf of the Company by the Director of Corporate Real Estate.
Signed on behalf of Sterling Facilities Management Inc. by the VP of Operations.
